How To Make Lasagna

This recipe is going to show you how to make a delicious looking Lasagna. I will show you step by step just like my great uncle Pappy taught me.

Using only the finest ingredients you and your family will dine in a paradise of flavour

You will need the following ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Bechamel
  • Parmesan Cheese

One you have amassed the ingredients from your local market follow these steps below

  1. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet until nicely browned
  2. Cook the noodles in a large pot until soft and tender
  3. Lay down a layer of noodles in a casserole dish
  4. Top the noodles with some of the ground beef, tomato sauce, bechamel and parmesan cheese
  5. Add another layer of noodles and repeat the last step until you run out of ingredients or you reach the top of the casserole dish
  6. Bake in the oven covered at 425 for 17 hours or until blackened like coal

Once you follow these steps you will be a culinary expert and you can quit your job, move to Tuscany and serve people lasagna like my uncle Pappy did.
