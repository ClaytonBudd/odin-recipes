How To Make Ramen Broth

This recipe is going to show you how to make a delicious looking ramen broth. I will show you step by step just like my foster mother Luk Wan Soo taught me.

Using only the finest ingredients you and your family will dine in a paradise of flavour

You will need the following ingredients

  • Chicken carcass
  • One large onion
  • Two large carrots
  • Three cloves garlic
  • One inch knob of ginger

One you have amassed the ingredients from your local market follow these steps below

  1. Place all the ingredients into a large stockpot
  2. Fill the stockpot with water until it almost covers the chicken
  3. Put the stockpot on the stove and bring to a simmer
  4. Simmer for 2-3 hours and remove any scum that rises to the top with a spoon
  5. After 2-3 hours strain the bones and vegetables from the broth and smell it to make sure it smells nice

Once you follow these steps you will be a culinary expert and you can quit your job and serve ramen broth to people on the street
